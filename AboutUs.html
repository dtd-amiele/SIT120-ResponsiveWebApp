WHO ARE WE ??
Web project is created for preschool- children (aged from 3 to 6). This website will provide friendly and funny environment for children to learn through many interesting games
The web project includes 6 games ( Mathematics, Puzzle, Brain Workout, Matching, Bubble, Color). Our games teach children how to do Maths as well as improve brain power in critical thinking and problem solving
